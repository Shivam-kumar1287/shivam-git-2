Delicious Pasta Recipe

This is a wonderful pasta recipe that you will absolutely love!

Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions.

    Time: 10 minutes

  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat and saut√© the garlic until fragrant.

    Time: 3 minutes

  3. Add the cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook until they start to soften.

    Time: 5 minutes

  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the tomatoes and garlic. Toss to combine.

    Time: 2 minutes

  5. Remove from heat and stir in the fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

    Time: 2 minutes

  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

    Time: 2 minutes
